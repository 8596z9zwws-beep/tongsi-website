Company Profile

Yunnan Tongsi Trading Co., Ltd. is a comprehensive enterprise specializing in hazardous chemicals and general cargo trade. Based in Yunnan, serving the whole country and radiating to Southeast Asia, we consistently adhere to the business philosophy of "Compliance, Quality First, Service Supreme".

We have established long-term strategic cooperation with multiple large-scale domestic chemical production bases, ensuring sufficient supply and timely delivery. We can provide customized supply solutions to meet diverse needs of different industries.

Business Scope

Hazardous Chemicals

We specialize in the trade of hazardous chemicals including Caustic Soda Solution, Hydrochloric Acid, Sodium Hypochlorite, Sulfuric Acid, Nitric Acid, Hydrogen Peroxide, etc. All products are sourced from licensed manufacturers and comply with national safety standards.

General Cargo Trade

In addition to hazardous chemicals, we also engage in general cargo trade including Laboratory Reagents, Electronic Grade Chemicals, and other industrial raw materials to meet diverse customer needs.

Customized Solutions

We provide customized supply solutions based on customer requirements, including specific concentrations, packaging methods, and delivery schedules. Our technical team can also provide product consultation and application guidance.
